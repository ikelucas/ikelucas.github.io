Blite

A game about alternating between creation and destruction to reach balance and achieve progress.



Link: Not available

Genre: 2D Platform / Puzzle

Summary: In a universe dominated by the colors black and white, representing the contrast between two extremes, the player must alternate control between two distinct realities with a character assigned to each - called Schwarz and Weiss - to advance through an environment that requires both the power of creation and destruction wielded by each character. It's also possible to encounter beings that don't belong to either reality, being necessary to avoid and overcome them in order to contain the corruption that breaks the balance between the power that creates and the power that destroys.
