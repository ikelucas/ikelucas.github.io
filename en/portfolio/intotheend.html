Into the End

A music album that mixes and explores various concepts with a specific composition by the band Linkin Park.



Link: Soundcloud
Genre: Mashup / Music Editing
Summary: A learning project in sound editing, mixing and music theory that seeks to apply different concepts and ideas in each of the thirteen songs produced amd released on the album; One way or another, all thirteen songs use the instrumental and/or the vocal track from the song In the End, by the band Linkin Park, as a common element to be reimagined or repurposed in each composition presented by the album.



Devlog

Development time: 3 months
Work duties: Mixing / Sound Editing / Composing
