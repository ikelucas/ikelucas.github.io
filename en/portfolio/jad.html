Just Another Day

A game about breaking a monotonous routine through drastic changes.



Link: Itch.io

Genre: Puzzle / Humorous

Summary: Using the mouse, the player controls a character who is always at home, stuck in a cycle performing tasks such as cleaning the house or doing chores on the computer, in a monotonous routine stemming from social distancing. It's possible to follow the daily schedule and keep every day predictable and boring, but the main goal is to find the absurd and definitive way to solve each of the present daily tasks, making them impossible to be done again the next day.
