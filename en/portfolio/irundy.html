Irundy - Guardiões da Floresta

A game about helping mythological beings to take care of the animals from the Amazon Rainforest and put out forest fires.



Link: Itch.io (download needed) (in Brazilian Portuguese)

Genre: Puzzle

Summary: With controls that resemble Tetris, the player needs to save the fauna and flora of the Amazon Rainforest, using pieces that can extinguish fire from the forests, winds that push flames away, or call Curupira himself, the brazilian myth, to help rescue lost animals. The main purpose of the game is to create a dialogue that addresses the criminal fires in the Amazon, and the importance of maintaining a healthy ecossystem without human intervention being destructive. The game and its proposed theme were awarded first place in the São Paulo region of the GameJam+ 2020 competition.

Other links: GameJam+ 2020 Winners of the Third Stage (in Brazilian Portuguese)
