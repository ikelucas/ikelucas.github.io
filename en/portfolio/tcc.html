L'Invention / Devaneio

Two sidescrolling 3D games developed as undergraduate projects for the Game Development college course at PUC-SP.



Link: Not available
Genre: 2D Platform / Stealth (L'Invention) / Auto Runner (Devaneio)
L'Invention Summary: The player controls the son of a famous inventor who was developing an undisclosed tool that was deemed revolutionary, but the development halted when the inventor died. The son decided to carry out his father's plans as a posthumous tribute, but to succeed he needs to break into a mansion protected by security cameras, guards on patrol and motion detectors, overcoming the obstacles he's facing to obtain a rare piece of machinery that is a key part of his father's project. You can run, crouch, jump, and hide on objects in the background, and navigating a non-linear environment you must obtain the item and escape without getting caught.
Devaneio Summary: After suffering a serious traffic accident, a young lover finds himself in a coma and struggling to regain consciousness. The player must control him through obstacles in a dream-like environment that mixes reality and fantasy, in order to regain his consciousness and return to his normal life. The player can jump and crouch, and must use combinations of these movements to avoid the imminent danger present in two unique stages with specific controls that represent aesthetics and ideas inside the boy's mind.
