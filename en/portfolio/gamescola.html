Videogame na Escola

A project that aims to use videogames as complement to help in the understanding of several subjects.



Genre: School Project
Summary: A school project that aims to use videogames as a direct and indirect reference to help students with different subjects. Games made with a specific educational purpose were used to complement and reinforce logical subjects, such as Literature and Maths, and more varied games were basis for theoretical subjects; the game Civilization was used as support for History, Geography and Sociology subjects, and references to the game The Last of Us were used to talk about Biology. Popular games such as Super Mario and Fortnite were used as backgrounds for logic problems, proposing greater student engagement by using terms and concepts familiar and present in their routines.
