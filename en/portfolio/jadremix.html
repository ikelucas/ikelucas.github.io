Just Another Day 2.0

A reimagining of the original game, making it more dynamic and with more possibilities.



Genre: Ação / Puzzle / Humorístico
Summary: A reimagining of the finished game Just Another Day, changing the camera style to first person, but maintaining the concept of going through a monotonous routine and being able to follow it or break it. The game's atmosphere has been rethought to make it more dynamic even in its most simple mode; it's possible to follow the routine as usual and receive a score based on the time taken to complete the tasks, adding a new competitive factor where there's a chance for players to compete against each other according to the game mode chosen. The humor and interaction between objects is maintained, with the addition of a performance grade for each level that allows for a more diverse choice of challenge and content.
