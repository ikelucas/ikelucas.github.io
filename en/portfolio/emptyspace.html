Empty Space

A game about navigating the vest space in search of answers and a clear path.



Link: Direct download

Genre: Adventure / Exploration

Summary: In your own spaceship, the player must navigate throught outer space using gravity to get around and reach points that can help find the way home. Enemies use derogatory messages to stop and suppress the player, and there is no equivalent way to attack, meaning the player must dodge the negative elements to move along the stars and asteroids trying to find the positive elements; the game seeks to be a metaphorical representation of the human mind when having thoughts of discouragement, lack of direction, or lack of confidence.

Other links: Game page on Global Game Jam 2021
